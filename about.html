Documentation:
As a develolper it is very important that you add documenetation to the project. Like comments for a line of code or a block of code to explain its purpose that your creating. It's best practice to add comments For ex. when you've created a class along with it's properties and methods rather than performing it later. Its easy to forget and its best to document while the material is still fresh in your memory.
